## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Persistence scope is intentionally unresolved
The local app persistence surface SHALL remain an exploration proposal until product and technical decisions define what data is stored, how it expires, and how it is communicated.

#### Scenario: Persistence idea is reviewed
- **WHEN** the persistent app data change is inspected
- **THEN** the proposal captures open questions without requiring implementation
- **AND** future design/spec/tasks artifacts can replace this exploration placeholder once decisions are made.
