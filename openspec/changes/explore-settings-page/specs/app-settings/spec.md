## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Settings scope is intentionally unresolved
The settings surface SHALL remain an exploration proposal until product decisions define its navigation model, scope, and persistence needs.

#### Scenario: Settings idea is reviewed
- **WHEN** the settings page change is inspected
- **THEN** the proposal captures open questions without requiring implementation
- **AND** future design/spec/tasks artifacts can replace this exploration placeholder once decisions are made.
